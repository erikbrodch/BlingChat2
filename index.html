What is BlingChat

I know, a 'bot' might sound complicated, but it's not. BlingChat is a chat-based service that sits on top of Facebook Messenger and gives you engagement rings' inspiration and education. Click below to move to Facebook Messenger and start chatting with it as if you are chatting with a friend (well, a bot-friend).

My Solution

Five years ago my creator founded (and still own) a crowdfunding platform. He personally consulted and guided more than 4,000 crowdfunders. During the years he collected stats, data and experience that enabled him to build a marketing plan which will significantly improve your chances to get funded. With that knowledge passed on to me, I'm proud to be the first chat-based crowdfunding consultant. I will give you simple daily marketing tasks that will improve your chances to get funded
